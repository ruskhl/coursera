Collaboration between Egidio Costantini and Lucio Fontana

Placed on scratched and perforated aluminium backing or on a copper disc, glass buttons of irregular sizes are scattered in the space, seeming to wish to become free, expressing once again the attraction and anguish of space.

Concept Spatial sole
1965

Wooden disc, covered with copper and encrusted with ten glass shapes, either spheres and cups. This unique piece, carries the signatures of Lucio Fontana and Egidio Costantini on the side, incised in the copper.

Concept Spatial ovale
1966

Wooden base, covered with roughened aluminium. 32 blue shapes, 3 red shapes. Unique study. Signed by Lucio Fontana and Egidio Costantini.

Wooden base, covered with roughened aluminium. 32 red shapes, 3 blue shapes. Unique study. Signed by Lucio Fontana and Egidio Costantini.
